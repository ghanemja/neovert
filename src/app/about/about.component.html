Our Mission

    To enable the democratization of large-scale physical advertising by:

  • Simplifying the physical advertising process
  • Near real-time physical advertising
  • Accessibility for any entity to publish (business, organization, resident)

FAQ

What does NeoVert mean?

NeoVert is a bridge between social media advertisement and traditional marketing methods. It is the way of the future for physical advertisements. Hence, the combination of "new" and "adVERTisements" leads to NeoVert!

Why was NeoVert founded?

NeoVert was founded to increase accessibility to physical advertising while also making the process simple and easy to use for any entity interested in advertising.

How does NeoVert work?

NeoBoards are physical billboards powered by digital LEDs to display images and videos. There is a three step process to posting an advertisement on a NeoBoard:

  • Create a NeoVert account and register your project.

  • Once a project is created, create an advertisement by uploading the ad and selecting the time frame and location you would like it to be posted to the NeoBoard.

  • Lastly, after ad safety has been validated for inappropriate or unethical content, the advertisement will be posted and analytics will be provided to the ad-poster to inform them of interactions.


Consumers of the advertisement are able to airdrop the advertisement to their phones simply by walking past a NeoBoard. For non iPhone users, QR codes will allow for access to links and related advertisement info.

Is NeoVert free?

NeoVert has two pricing options. The first is a pay per post, which is based on the location of the NeoBoard and the duration of time the ad is posted. The second option is a monthly / annual subscription.

What are the benefits of NeoVert?

NeoVert simplifies the advertising process. Now, you can post to physical billboards about anything ranging from a lost pet to a business venture - just with the click of a button. This allows for anyone to have a platform to share, review, like and comment on advertisements.
